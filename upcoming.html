All that you need to know about Badhte Raho India

"Badhte Raho India" is an ideation-stage entrepreneurship event for students currently in college who have an entrepreneur idea in their mind or an early-stage startup. If you win, you will be given direct funding from the VCs via Pnapaao.Also, Pnapaao will provide you with all the consulting and marketing services, such as getting registration licenses, mentoring, formulating business plans, legal services, making pitch decks, and website building to convert your idea into a successful startup. More than 500 colleges and 20 VCs will be a part of this mega-event, so register now and take advantage of this opportunity if you have a mind of an entrepreneur.Badhte Raho India is a Pan-India level competition to promote and support budding entrepreneurs by setting up their startups and providing them consulting services such as mentorship, guidance, core-team building, acquiring licenses, and financial support to stabilise their startup and broaden their reach.

To participate in the competition the participants would be required to submit a provisonal pesentation (maximum two slides), preferably via MS Powerpoint file (or google slides), regarding the problem they are working on.This is merely a test of your motivation and comprehension of your idea, therefore it does not need to be a perfect pitch deck.

Badhte Raho India: Stages and Timelines

Prelims Round
28 Jan 23 to 31 Jan 23

Pitch Deck Making Session: Provisionally submitted proposals will be invited to a pitch-deck development session where they will learn how to present their idea. After the session, participants will have two days to submit their pitch deck in the format presented in the session. Mentors would assist participants throughout the two days.

Advisory Round
03 Feb 23 to 09 Feb 23

The Round 1 winners will be questioned about their pitch decks' customer segment, target market, value proposition, etc. Teams must conduct considerable market research beforehand. The advisory group includes 20-year startup ecosystem veterans. Teams must make suggested changes and resubmit their pitch deck within 2 days.

Pitching Round
10 Feb 23 to 14 Feb 23

How to Pitch 101 - Shortlisted candidates will learn how to pitch their deck to venture capitalists and investors. Final Pitch - Teams will pitch their concepts to venture capitalists under timer.
